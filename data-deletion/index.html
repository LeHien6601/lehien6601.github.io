Data Deletion Request – LNHGames

If you would like to request deletion of your data for the game Emoji Sort: Match & Pop, please follow the steps below.

How to request data deletion

  1. Send an email to: lnhdev2@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your Player ID (if available)
    • Your device information (optional)
Tip: Providing your Player ID helps us process your request faster.

What data will be deleted

  • Your game progress (cloud save data)
  • Any associated identifiers used within the game

What data may be retained

  • Some data may be retained temporarily for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes

Processing time

We will process your request within 7 days.

Important note

This request will delete your saved progress associated with Google Play Games Services. Some data may also be managed by Google according to their own policies.
